Starbucks

Starbucks has been committed to ethically sourcing and roasting the highest quality arabica coffee in the world. It was here where Starbucks opened its first store, offering fresh-roasted coffee beans, tea and spices from around the world for our customers to take home. Our name was inspired by the classic tale, “Moby-Dick,” evoking the seafaring tradition of the early coffee traders.

Nescafe

Nescafé is a brand of coffee made by Nestlé. It comes in many different forms. The name is a portmanteau of the words "Nestlé" and "café". Nestlé first introduced their flagship coffee brand in Switzerland on 1 April 1938. It's a delicious blend that comes to you from some of the world's finest coffee beans. And by blending those with milk, you get the texture and flavor so many people enjoy each day. Nescafé coffee is a rich, smooth coffee with a distinctive taste and aroma.

Barista

Today, Barista is one of the lucrative jobs around the world. And in Nepal, particularly at this time, when youths have been hit hard by the harsh unemployment simply getting in-hand knowledge of making coffee could be the trouble-shooter for you. Himalayan Espresso Barista Training course is aimed for both the aspirants and those already working in the coffee industry. The course will help you master on how to make the prefect espresso and the drinks that escort its base. This course is also beneficial for those who have just started using the espresso machine. At Himalayan Espresso, we will not only teach you on how to prepare espresso based beverages in a precise way for better taste, but also on how to attract and sell them to customers.

About

Himalayan Espresso is your one stop shop for all things coffee in Kathmandu! The secret to great tasting coffee begins with the right beans. Our hand-picked 100 % Arabica beans are grown and roasted right here locally in Nepal. Another requisite for exquisite coffee is the right expresso machine. At Himalayan Espresso, we import the best coffee machines straight from the Mecca of Western coffeeItaly! Of-course the best coffee beans and coffee machines are useless unless they are in the hands of a professional barista. So, our own company Fresh Cup is right here to brew and serve you fresh coffee.Our professionally trained baristas share our passion for great coffee. As proponents of great coffee, we dont think their availability should be limited to the Fresh cup coffee. In order to make great coffee available elsewhere, we offer barista training classes, supply imported coffee machines and parts, and even offer consulting services on owning and running your very own coffee shop in Nepal. So there you have it, the ingredients to amazing coffee include the best of beans, man, and machine.
